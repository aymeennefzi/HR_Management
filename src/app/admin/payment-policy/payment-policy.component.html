Tax Rate (%)

Percentage of gross salary deducted as income tax.

Example: 14% of 1000 dinars = 140 dinars of tax.

Social Security Rate (%)

Percentage of gross salary deducted as social security contribution.

Finances healthcare, family allowances, etc.

Example: 14% of 1000 dinars = 140 dinars of contribution.

Other Deductions (dinars)

Fixed amount of deductions not tied to percentages.

Includes union dues, loan repayments, etc.

Example: 150 dinars deducted in addition to percentages.

Payment Day

Day when salaries are paid to employees.

Example: Payment on the 22nd of each month.

Excess Day Payment

Payment per day for excess leave days.

Example: 5 % per excess day.

Allowed Days

Number of days allowed for each type of leave.

  • Bereavement: Leave for family death.
  • Maternity: Maternity leave.
  • Paternity: Paternity leave.
  • RTT: Compensatory rest days.
  • Sick Leave: Sick leave.
  • Unpaid leave: Leave without pay.
  • Paid leave: Standard paid leave.
